## claude-status-bar — Claude spark / logo / "Clawd" crab artwork

The Claude thinking-spark frames, the resting Claude logo, and the "Clawd" crab
walk-cycle frames are bundled (as base64-encoded PNGs) from **claude-status-bar**:

- Project: https://github.com/m1ckc3s/claude-status-bar
- Files in this repo:
  - `Sources/Pookify/Assets/ClaudeSparkFrames.swift`
  - `Sources/Pookify/Assets/ClaudeLogoFrame.swift`
  - `Sources/Pookify/Assets/ClaudeCrabFrames.swift`

**Modifications:** the crab walk frames have been modified — the eye pair was recentered so the
eyes don't drift to the leading edge during the walk cycle.
